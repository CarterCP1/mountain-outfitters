About Mountain Outfitters

Serving the Northern Kettle Moraine since the spring of 1994.

1994 Est. West Bend, WI

Who We Are

Welcome to Mountain Outfitters

Mountain Outfitters offers a wide range of skiing, snowboarding, and biking products and services. Our professionally trained staff can answer questions about equipment as well as where to ride or ski locally — no matter what your skill level.

Located in historic downtown West Bend, Mountain Outfitters is conveniently close to downhill skiing at Sunburst Ski Area and cross country skiing in the Northern Kettle Moraine Forest at Zillmer Trails, Greenbush Trails, and New Fane Trails.

Mountain Outfitters is located less than one-quarter mile from the Eisenbahn State Bike Trail and is just minutes from the mountain bike trails and scenic roads of the Northern Kettle Moraine State Forest. Maps and trail descriptions are available online and in the store.

Our Services

What We Do

Full-service sales, repair, and rentals — all under one roof in downtown West Bend.

Ski Shop Wide range of sales, repair, and high-performance tuning services for skis and snowboards.
Rental Shop Downhill skis, cross country skis, and snowshoes. Daily, weekend, weekly, and seasonal rates.
Bike Shop All bikes purchased include a one-year free service guarantee — all labor free for the first year.
Custom Bikes Custom bicycles from Moots, Kent Erickson, and Ventana built from the frame up.
Team BONK We sponsor Team BONK, competing in WORS and WEMS. Team rides are open to everyone.
Community Rides Check out the Washington County Bicycle Club for group rides and community events.
