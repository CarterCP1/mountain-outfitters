Our Story

The Northern Kettle Moraine Is Our Backyard

We've been serving the Northern Kettle Moraine since the spring of 1994. Mountain Outfitters started as a place where passionate riders and skiers could get real advice from people who actually spend their weekends on the trails — and that hasn't changed.

Located in historic downtown West Bend, we're less than a quarter mile from the Eisenbahn State Bike Trail and just minutes from the mountain bike trails of the Northern Kettle Moraine State Forest.

Ski Sales & Tuning Wide selection, expert fitting, high-performance service
Bike Sales & Service 1-year free service guarantee on every bike
Rental Shop Downhill, XC ski & snowshoe rentals
Custom Bikes Moots, Kent Erickson & Ventana builds
