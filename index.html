Our Story

The Northern Kettle Moraine Is Our Backyard

We've been serving the Northern Kettle Moraine since the spring of 1994. Mountain Outfitters started as a place where passionate riders and skiers could get real advice from people who actually spend their weekends on the trails — and that hasn't changed.

Our professionally trained staff can answer questions about equipment as well as where to ride or ski locally — no matter what your skill level. Maps and trail descriptions are available both online and in the store.

Located in historic downtown West Bend, we're less than a quarter mile from the Eisenbahn State Bike Trail and just minutes from the mountain bike trails and scenic roads of the Northern Kettle Moraine State Forest. We also sponsor Team BONK, a racing team competing in the Wisconsin Off-Road Series (WORS) and the Wisconsin Endurance Mountain Bike Series (WEMS).
