Ski Shop

Everything You Need on the Mountain

The Mountain Outfitters ski shop offers a wide range of sales, repair, and high-performance tuning services. Our staff ski the local terrain and can fit you to the right equipment for your ability and the conditions you'll encounter.

Need to rent before you commit? Our rental shop has you covered with downhill skis, cross country skis, and snowshoes. Call (262) 335-0424 for current pricing.
