Bike Shop

The Mountain Outfitters Bike Advantage

Every bike purchased at Mountain Outfitters comes with a one-year free service guarantee — all labor on repairs is free for the first full year, backed by the manufacturer's frame and component warranties. That means your new bike will be professionally serviced for free for the first year you own it.

We also offer custom bicycle builds from Moots, Kent Erickson, and Ventana — built from the frame up for high-performance riding and racing. Call us at (262) 335-0424 for pricing and current inventory.
